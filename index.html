metro-pedestrian-couting

发表于 2018-07-09 |

Intro

Recently, I’m working on a metro pedestrain counting project.
By counting how many people have entered the metro, the metro station can keep track of this and control the traffic flow

Procedure

I’ve tested YOLOv2 & YOLOv3 by annotated the pedestrain myself.
Use the darknet to train, and also test the pytorch-yolo-v3 results.
then, I use the SORT method which is the opensource baseline for MOT benchmark.
